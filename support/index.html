Bubble VS Support

If you need help with account, gameplay, purchases, or bug reports, contact us here.

Contact

Email: support@bubblevs.app

Please include your device model, iOS/iPadOS version, app version, and screenshots if possible.

Common Issues

  • In-App Purchase not showing: open app and wait 10 seconds, then retry.
  • In-App Purchase pending: check sandbox account/payment status and retry.
  • Restore purchase: go to Profile → Restore Purchase.

Privacy

Bubble VS uses Game Center and StoreKit for matchmaking and purchases. No private purchase credentials are stored in app.
